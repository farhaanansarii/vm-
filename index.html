Meet Abhishek Tiwari

Abhishek Tiwari is the Founder & Chief Mentor of Venture Mozart, with over 22 years of
global experience in startup growth, innovation, and policy advisory across 25+
countries. He’s the architect behind
Uttar Pradesh’s first Startup Policy, the force behind the StartInUP brand, and has guided 14,000+ startups and 70+ incubators.

A former Startup Advisory Lead at KPMG India and a Certified Independent Director,
Abhishek has advised 100+ governments and multinationals, blending corporate insight
with deep ecosystem impact. Through Venture Mozart, he continues to mentor
founders, family businesses, and policymakers to drive innovation and sustainable
growth.
